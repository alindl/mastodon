    What is koyu.space?

    koyu.space is an open source social network. Similar to Twitter or Tumblr, users can make profiles, post messages, images or videos, and of course follow other users. Messages follow a 800 character limit and are displayed in a chronological order. Unlike other platforms, however, koyu.space is decentralized, meaning that there is no one server, company or person running it. While other social media platforms are owned and operated by a single corporation who has full control over everything, anyone can create and run their own server of koyu.space.
